Executive margin review

Source: internal brief

Operating margin improved by 4.2 points in 2025 after procurement consolidated vendors and support teams automated repetitive intake work.

January 2025 established a baseline for vendor spend. April 2025 introduced renewal governance. September 2025 locked the new support automation workflow.

Procurement versus support shows different drivers: procurement delivered contract savings, while support delivered productivity gains.

Recommendation: keep renewal governance monthly and expand support automation to the enterprise queue.
