Key Drivers

The East region continues to generate the highest absolute revenue at $38,200, though its growth rate has plateaued. The West region shows the strongest momentum, with repeat buyers growing 18% month-over-month and a 34% retention rate.

North and South regions present a mixed picture: North shows steady order growth (+6% MoM) but lower average order values, while South's revenue declined 3% in March after a strong January launch.
